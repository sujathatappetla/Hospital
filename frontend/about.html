Since then it has developed into a reputed and unique center for Cardiology, Neurology and also added medical and surgical specialties under one roof providing advanced medical care facilities. It is managed by 800+ strong committed staff including highly qualified full time medical specialists, experienced nursing staff, efficient technicians, trained paramedical staff, Physical therapists, social workers and fully computerized administrative department working towards the comprehensive care of patients.
